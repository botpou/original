How to Connect

  1. Open WhatsApp and go to settings
  2. Select "Linked Devices"
  3. Click on "Link a device"
  4. Click "Link With Phone Number" instead
  5. Copy and Paste the pairing code you saw in the web
  6. Wait a few seconds for the device to be connected
  7. And now your bot will be Active
